Seele mining reward
===================

A miner gets reward for mining a block. We define one “Seele year” as
the time needed to produce 3,150,000 blocks. In different “years”, the
mining reward differs.

================ ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ======
\                Year 1 Year 2 Year 3 Year 4 Year 5 Year 6 Year 7 Year 8 Year 9
================ ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ======
**block reward** 6      4      3      2.5    2      2      1.5    1.5    1.5
================ ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ====== ======

(the reward unit: seele).
